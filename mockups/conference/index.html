What we've built

Static HTML mockups for the IECA Baltimore booth conversations (May 18). Single cohesive design family — light cool-gray content, dark slate sidebar, indigo accent, a single sans-serif typography family (Geist). Each surface answers one focused "what is this page for" question.

Consultant Today

NEW · top-of-nav homepage

A new top-level surface above Students in the left nav. Aggregates the things needing attention across the whole roster — overdue, today's meetings, flagged follow-ups, due today, snapshots awaiting review, modules due today — ordered by urgency. "Not just a per-student overview, but your today… your overview of all the things that need your attention." — Roei, 5/12. Building for V1 conference to test whether the pattern resonates with IECA attendees as solving a real pain point.

Today · attention triage
A consultant orienting on the day — what needs my attention right now across the whole roster, ordered by urgency.
Single-column feed: six widgets stacked by urgency (Overdue → Today's meetings → Flagged · follow-up → Due today → New snapshots · awaiting review → Module check-ins · due today). Compact TickTick-style rows, priority-encoded left-bar always paired with text status pill (D49). Every row quick-links to per-student detail; every widget head links to the relevant surface. Round 2: full viewport width, rose reserved for TRUE overdue only (Awaiting / Stuck / recency markers → amber or neutral), meeting times stripped of color, snapshot rows unified to neutral priority-bar, "+more" aggregator replaced with explicit "View all" link, Meetings moved above Flagged per "what am I doing today" framing.
