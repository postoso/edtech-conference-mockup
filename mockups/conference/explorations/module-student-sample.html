When ordering activities on the Common App, which factor should drive your top three slots?

Chronological order — most recent first, oldest last
Strategic priority — the activities that best evidence your narrative thesis
Hours per week — the activities with the highest time commitment
Awards earned — the activities with the most recognition
Right. Admissions readers spend the most time on your top 3 — those slots should be the ones that argue hardest for your narrative thesis, not the ones that happen to be most recent.
